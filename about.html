The Journey

Will Woods is a former music educator and musician from Marietta, Georgia. After splitting time for several years between music and as a caretaker for a family member, Will and his wife moved to Peachtree Corners near the end of 2018. Teaching band and working professionally as a trumpet player has prepared Will for life as a developer through the rigorous time management and techincal skills required and the sheer amount of time and effort it takes to harvest the fruits of those labors. Will recieved his first commission for the development of a web application 174 days after writing his first line of code.
